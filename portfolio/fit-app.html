Introduction

Fit App enables users to monitor and track their weight and lifting numbers over time. I enjoyed developing this application since it encourages a healthy lifestyle and incorporates data visualization. This application was developed using the Laravel framework.

Each user can track their own weight each day, along with the date. The same goes for their three main lifts: squat, deadlift, and bench press. Statistics are generated from their main lifts, such as their 1 Rep Maximum and where they stand in terms of their strength relative to other people their size.

Initial Ideas

I initially wanted to make an application that would help me track my own weightlifting data, but as time went on, I knew I should extend it to help me track my weight as well.

This was the first time I had worked with the D3.js graphing library, but I learned a lot. The graphs used in this application were quite simple, but making them animated was a good learning process for me. Data values are plotted on the chart, and tooltips are displayed when hovering over the points.

Final Concept

I ended up making an application that would chart a user's strength in regards to their own lifts and weight. Also, it was my first time developing an application that uses Material Design. I'd like to re-visit this project and add more complicated and stylized charts, as well as re-design the UI since there was a lack of knowledge on my part with Material Design practices.
